Light Sculpture

About this project

The Light Sculpture features clear acrylic rods that internally reflect the light from LEDs inserted into their bases. Students will choose their own rod length, rod placement, and LED colors to create a unique aesthetic.

What you'll learn

  • Part creation using relations and dimensioning
  • Advanced assemblies using efficient mates
  • Large scale exploded views and bill-of-materials
  • Defining and organizing complex sketches
